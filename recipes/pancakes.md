# Pancakes

Ingredients
* 225 gr | White Flour
* 215 ml | Milk
* 8 gr | Baking Powder
* 145 gr | Sourdough starter
* 40 gr | Sugar
* 9 gm | Salt

- Knead
- Proof for 2 hours outside or overnight in the fridge
- Push out the air, Knead a bit more.
- Proof for 1 hour.
- Bake at 200 for 30 to 35 minutes
