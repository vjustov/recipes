# Sourdough bread

Ingredients
* 400 gr | White Flour
* 230 ml | Water | Tepid
* 160 gr | Sourdough Starter
* 5 gm | Salt

- Knead until ready
- Proof for 3 hours
- Push out the air, Knead a bit more. Roll into a ball, put into a supporting enclosure.
- Proof for 3 more hours. 
- Bake at high temp, at more than 230 C for 30 to 35 minutes.
